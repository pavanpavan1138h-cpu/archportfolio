The Glass Pavilion

Location Oslo, Norway
Year 2023
Size 12,000 sq ft
Client City of Oslo

The Concept

The Glass Pavilion is a striking cultural center that acts as a transparent beacon, welcoming the public into a space of art and interaction.

Designed to be as unobtrusive as possible, the structural framework is minimized, creating an illusion of a floating roof above a continuous pane of glass. This ensures that the beautiful surrounding landscape and the exhibits inside are visually merged.

Advanced environmental controls were integrated seamlessly, ensuring that despite the expansive glazing, the building remains highly energy-efficient throughout the harsh Scandinavian winters and bright summers.
