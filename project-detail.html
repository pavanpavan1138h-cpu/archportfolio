Villa Nova

Location Carmel, California
Year 2024
Size 4,500 sq ft
Client Private

The Concept

Villa Nova is a modern coastal retreat designed to blur the boundaries between indoor living and the rugged natural landscape.

Situated on a steep cliffside, the architecture adapts to the topography rather than fighting it. The structure is composed of three distinct pavilions connected by glass corridors, allowing the surrounding native flora to weave through the home's footprint.

We utilized a constrained material palette of board-formed concrete, weathered cedar, and expansive high-performance glazing. This ensures the building ages gracefully while withstanding the harsh coastal elements.
